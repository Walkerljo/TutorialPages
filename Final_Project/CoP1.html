Constant of Proportionality

Links Reference
  • WalkerWeb Tutorials

If you have a direct variation, the constant of proportionality is a number that represents the ratio of one number to another.

Example Question

If you have 3 gerbils, they eat 2 oz of food a day, and 6 gerbils eat 2 oz of food.

Next Step

Write as a ratio #gerbils/ oz food
6 gerbils / 4 oz

Last Step

Simplify;
6 gerbils/ 4 oz = 3 gerbils/2oz
= 3/2
